Designer + Developer

I've been a web professional for well over 10 years, spending the last 9 years of that at Sonnax Transission Company (formerly Sonnax Industries). In that time I've gone from the print side of design, creating custom posters, ads and catalogs, to front-end design, to front-end development and finally to back end design. This continual growth has been spurred by always wanting to put out the best possible product and learning the technologies I would need to accomplish that aim.
